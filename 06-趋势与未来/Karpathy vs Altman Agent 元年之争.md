# Karpathy vs Altman Agent 元年之争

## Altman 的乐观宣言

2025 年 1 月，Sam Altman 在博客文章《Reflections》中写道：

> "We believe that, in 2025, we may see **the first AI agents join the workforce** and materially change the output of companies."

同时宣称 OpenAI "已经知道如何构建 AGI"。

## Karpathy 的冷水

2025 年 10 月，Karpathy 在 Dwarkesh Podcast 上直言不讳：

> **"They just don't work."**
>
> "They don't have enough intelligence, they're not multimodal enough, they can't do computer use and all this stuff. They don't have continual learning. You can't just tell them something and they'll remember it. They're cognitively lacking and it's just not working."

核心论点：不应该谈论 **"Year of the Agent"**，而应该是 **"Decade of the Agent"**。他认为当前的大语言模型在工具调用、多模态和持续学习方面仍有根本性缺陷。

## Karpathy 的自我定位

> "My AI timelines are **about 5-10X pessimistic** compared to what you'll find in your neighborhood SF AI house party or on your twitter timeline, **but still quite optimistic w.r.t. a rising tide of AI deniers and skeptics**."

既不是硅谷的盲目乐观，也不是否认 AI 潜力的悲观。Agent 会到来，但需要一个**十年**，而非一年。

## 现实数据

数据更支持 Karpathy 的谨慎：仅 11% 组织有 Agent 在生产环境中运行，顶级模型在 APEX-Agents 基准上首次完成率不到 25%。然而 [[OpenClaw 社区热度总览|社区增长数据]] 却讲述了另一个故事——Karpathy 说还要 5-10 年，但 237K GitHub Stars 和 127 万周下载量暗示 Agent 时代可能已经到来，至少在开发者社区是如此。
